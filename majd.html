perfect guacamole recipe

Ingredients:2 ripe avocadoes,1/2 red onion,2 tablespoons of cilantro leaves finely chopped,1 tablespoon of fresh lime, 1/2 teaspoon of coarse salt, a dash of grated black pepper,1/2 ripe tomato seeds and pulp removed chopped,serve with tortilla chips ...

Instructions: cut the avocado and mash it up add the chopped onion cilantro lime salt and pepper keep the tomatoes separate until ready to serve add the chopped tomatoes and then mix...
