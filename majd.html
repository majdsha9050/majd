perfect guacamole recipe

Ingredients:2 ripe avocadoes, 1/2 red onion, 2 tablespoons of cilantro leaves finely chopped, 1 tablespoon of fresh lime, 1/2 teaspoon of coarse salt, a dash of grated black pepper, 1/2 ripe tomato seeds and pulp removed chopped, serve with tortilla chips

Instructions: cut the avocado and mash it up add the chopped onion cilantro lime salt and pepper keep the tomatoes separate until ready to serve add the chopped tomatoes and then mix

Strawberry banana smoothie

Ingredients: two bananas a small punnet of strawberries 1 cup or orange juice handfull of ice half a punnet of blueberries (optional)

Instructions: cut the bananas into small slices and cut the leaves off the strawberries and then wash the strawberries, put both of the bananas and strawberries into a blender and add a cup of organge juice and blueberries if wanted and then add the handfull of ice and blend till soft and then enjoy
